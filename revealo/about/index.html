About Revealo

Revealo is a picture reveal logic puzzle built as a Progressive Web App.

リビーロは、数字と色のヒントから隠れた絵を当てるPWAゲームです。短時間で遊べるスマホ向けロジックパズルとして開発しています。

Game concept

Players solve grid clues, reveal image cells, and try to guess the picture as early as possible.

Platform

The app runs in modern browsers and can be installed from Safari or Chrome as a PWA.
