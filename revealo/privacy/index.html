Privacy Policy

This policy explains how Revealo handles data.

Last updated: May 20, 2026

Information we store

Revealo stores puzzle progress, completion status, hints used, mistakes, and tutorial status in your browser's local storage. This data stays on your device and is used to resume gameplay.

Account and personal information

Revealo does not require account registration. We do not ask for your name, email address, or payment information inside the game.

Advertising

Revealo may display ads provided by Google AdSense. Google and its partners may use cookies or similar technologies to serve and measure ads. You can learn more about Google advertising privacy at Google's advertising policy pages.

Analytics

At this stage, Revealo does not use a custom analytics system. If analytics are added later, this policy will be updated.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us from the Contact page.
